# GEO, AEO & Technical SEO Consulting

## Future-proof your discoverability with expert optimization for AI-powered search, answer engines, and traditional SEO

### The search landscape has fundamentally changed

Google AI Overviews, ChatGPT, Perplexity, and other AI platforms have transformed how people discover information. Click-through rates are falling, citations are replacing rankings, and most organizations lack the technical depth — spanning search expertise and software engineering — to compete for visibility in this new landscape.

I treat discoverability as a data problem, not a guessing game. I build strategies — backed by SQL analysis, structured data, and hands-on implementation — that work across traditional search engines, generative AI platforms, and answer engines.

## How I Can Help

I work at every level, from hands-on SQL analysis to strategic content planning, across five areas:

### Generative Engine Optimization (GEO)

Ensure your content appears in AI overviews, ChatGPT responses, and other generative search results. I analyze how AI systems surface information, then architect your content to maximize visibility in these emerging channels.

**Includes:** AI visibility assessment, content gap analysis, structured data implementation (Schema.org, JSON-LD), content rewriting for AI comprehension, and a measurement framework for tracking AI appearances.

### Answer Engine Optimization (AEO)

Optimize for featured snippets, knowledge panels, and conversational search. I map the questions your audience is asking and structure content to provide authoritative, AI-friendly answers.

**Includes:** question-intent mapping, snippet and position-zero optimization, FAQ/Q&A content development, and schema markup for rich results and knowledge graphs.

### Technical SEO Audits & Implementation

Deep-dive audits using SQL analysis, Python scripts, and industry-leading tools, followed by prioritized recommendations and hands-on implementation support.

**Includes:** crawlability and indexation audits, site architecture review, Core Web Vitals optimization, structured data validation, and log file analysis of crawler behavior.

### Analytics & Performance Measurement

Custom analytics frameworks — built with SQL, Python, and visualization tools — that track AI visibility, snippet performance, and ROI across every discovery channel.

**Includes:** AI/answer-engine tracking, dashboard design (Looker Studio, Tableau, or custom), KPI frameworks tied to business objectives, and attribution modeling across discovery channels.

### Content Strategy & Architecture

Content structures that work for both human readers and AI systems.

**Includes:** content inventory and audit, taxonomy and metadata strategy, information architecture redesign, and editorial guidelines for AI-optimized content creation.
